/en-US/docs/Glossary/Color_space	The CIELAB (or CIELab) color space, also referred to as L*a*b* (or Lab* for short), represents the entire range of color that humans can see. This color space was defined by International Commission on Illumination (CIE). It expresses color as three values: L* for perceptual lightness, and a* and b* for the four unique colors of human vision: red, green, blue, and yellow.
